Courses

MA-109 - Calculus

This is a very interesting and challenging course as students are introduced to mathematical rigour for the first time after their JEE days. I found this course to be a little challenging during the initial days, but as it progressed, things became simpler and easy to understand.

MA-111 - Calculus

This is a followup course on MA109. Here, concepts on double integration, multiple integration, green's theorem, etc. are introduced to students.

CH-105 - Organic and Inorganic Chemistry

In this introductory course, I studied two very important divisions of chemistry. I found this course to be really stimulating as many new concepts such as MOT, Zeeman Splitting, Ligand and Crystal Field Theory and many other new concepts.

PH-107 - Quantum Physics and Application

In this course, we are introduced to Quantum Physics and several new concepts such as the Schrodinger Equation while concepts such as Black Body Radiation, Planck's Law, etc. were recapped.
